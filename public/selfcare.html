Self-Care Tips

Practical advice for maintaining health throughout the seasons

Summer Health Tips

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water
  • Wear light, breathable clothing
  • Avoid direct sunlight during peak hours
  • Eat fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Use sunscreen to protect your skin

Monsoon Health Tips

  • Keep your surroundings clean to prevent waterborne diseases
  • Use mosquito repellents and nets
  • Avoid walking in stagnant water
  • Keep food covered and fresh
  • Boost immunity with vitamin C rich foods

Winter Health Tips

  • Dress warmly to prevent colds
  • Maintain indoor humidity
  • Eat warm, nourishing foods
  • Stay physically active
  • Get adequate sleep

General Self-Care Tips

  • Regular physical activity improves overall health
  • Balanced diet with seasonal fruits and vegetables
  • Maintain personal and environmental hygiene
  • Adequate sleep and rest for recovery
  • Practice stress management and mindfulness
