Influenza (Flu)

A contagious respiratory illness caused by influenza viruses.

Symptoms: Fever, cough, sore throat, body aches, fatigue
Prevention: Annual flu vaccine, hand washing, avoiding close contact with sick people

Common Cold

A viral infection of the upper respiratory tract.

Symptoms: Runny nose, sore throat, cough, congestion, sneezing
Prevention: Hand washing, avoiding touching face, staying away from sick people

Malaria

A mosquito-borne infectious disease.

Symptoms: Fever, chills, headache, nausea, muscle pain
Prevention: Mosquito nets, insect repellents, antimalarial medication

Dengue Fever

A mosquito-borne viral infection.

Symptoms: High fever, severe headache, pain behind eyes, joint and muscle pain
Prevention: Mosquito control, wearing long sleeves, using repellents

Seasonal Diarrhea

Often caused by contaminated food or water during monsoon.

Symptoms: Frequent loose stools, abdominal pain, dehydration
Prevention: Safe drinking water, proper food hygiene, hand washing

Heatstroke

A serious heat-related illness that occurs when body temperature rises dangerously high.

Symptoms: High body temperature, confusion, rapid heartbeat, loss of consciousness
Prevention: Stay hydrated, avoid direct sunlight during peak hours, wear light clothing, take frequent breaks in shade
Remedies: Move to a cool place, drink water slowly, use cold packs on neck and armpits, seek medical help immediately
Self-care: Rest in cool environment, monitor body temperature, avoid strenuous activities
Nutrition: Consume electrolyte-rich drinks, eat light, hydrating foods like watermelon and cucumber, avoid caffeine and alcohol

Dehydration

A condition that occurs when you lose more fluids than you take in.

Symptoms: Dry mouth and skin, fatigue, dizziness, dark urine
Prevention: Drink plenty of water, avoid alcohol and caffeine, eat water-rich foods, limit outdoor activities during heat
Remedies: Drink oral rehydration solution, rest in cool place, sip water slowly, seek medical attention if severe
Self-care: Monitor urine color, weigh yourself daily, avoid overheating
Nutrition: Consume fruits with high water content, electrolyte supplements, light, watery soups
